Pump Fakes

So if you hav learned how to shoot, then you should learn how to pump fake. Pump fake is when you pretend to shoot but you hold back,
and then the second time you shoot the ball. This trick is used so you can waste the defender's energy to try to block you again.
A defender will normally try to block you when he see's you going for a shot, so what you will have to do is hold the ball in the
same form and then instead of shooting, you pump fake, and then the second time you shoot the ball so you have a better loot
for the hoop. Many people use this trick for tricking the defender about when they will shoot, but overusing it can be bad too.
Overusing this trick will let the defender know what your plan is, and he will be more aware about what you will try to do.
